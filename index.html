Veteran-Led Transitional Housing Program

Professional Transitional Housing & Comprehensive Support Services

Hodges Haven for Heroes provides structured transitional housing and comprehensive support services for veterans and their families in Marion, Arkansas. Our evidence-based program model facilitates successful reintegration into civilian life through housing stability, skills development, and career readiness.

Leadership Statement

Founded and operated by veterans Monte and Rhonda Hodges, our organization delivers proven transitional support programming. With combined decades of military service and community development experience, our leadership team understands the unique challenges veterans face post-service and has designed a comprehensive model to address housing instability, employment barriers, and social reintegration.

Program Fees & Financial Accessibility

Hodges Haven for Heroes maintains an income-based fee structure to ensure program accessibility regardless of financial circumstances.

Sliding Scale Fee Model

Individual program fees are assessed during the intake process based on income verification and financial capability. Our fee structure is designed to remove financial barriers while maintaining program sustainability.

Standard Program Rate:

$2,000/month

Comprehensive package includes housing, utilities, meals, facility access, and all programming services

Financial assistance available for eligible participants through our income-based fee reduction program.

Facility Location

Our Community Care Homes are strategically located in Marion, Arkansas, directly off Interstate 55, providing easy access from Memphis and the broader Mid-South region. The facility operates within the Comfort Inn property, offering professional accommodations and amenities in a supportive residential setting.
